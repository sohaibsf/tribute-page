Michael Jackson

Recognized as the artist most successful of all time, is a leading figure in the history of the entertainment industry and a major cultural icons of the xx th century.
The seventh in a family of nine children , he sang with his brothers from the age of six and began a professional career at the age of eleven in the Jackson Five , a group formed with his older brothers.
While remaining a member of the group (until 1984), in 1971 he began a solo career.

Media engagements and relations:

1985 - We Are the World , to raise funds to fight famine in Ethiopia .
1991 - Heal the World , aimed at raising funds for the fight against famine, poverty and childhood illnesses
1993 – Gone Too Soon, chanson en hommage à Ryan White, un jeune garçon victime du sida
